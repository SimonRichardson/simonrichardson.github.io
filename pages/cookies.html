Cookies & Privacy

This website does not track you.

  • No analytics or tracking scripts are used.
  • No third-party cookies are set.
  • No personal data is collected or transmitted.

The only cookies stored are first-party preference cookies used to remember your settings (e.g. window positions, dark mode choice). These are never shared with anyone and exist solely to improve your experience on return visits.

You can clear these at any time via your browser settings.
